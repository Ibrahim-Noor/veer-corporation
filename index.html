ABOUT US

We are an Indenting House as well as an Importer dealing in Paper, Paperboard & Packaging Materials having rich working experience of over 50 years. We are representing reputed manufacturers globally. We are well connected in the Industrial, Commercial, Public and Private Sectors. We have sales offices in Karachi and Lahore, both Business Hubs of Pakistan managed by professionally educated managers.

We are looking for Mills & / or Suppliers who have representation interest in Pakistan / Afghanistan market. For all your queries and questions, please feel free to contact us.

We are also keenly interested to cater and to associate in product development of industrial consumers.

We efficiently & confidentially source quality with reliability to the satisfaction of buyers. We solicit co-operation and support of buyers for inquires and feedback.
