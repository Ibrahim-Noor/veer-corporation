ABOUT US

We are an Indenting House as well as an Importer dealing in Paper, Paperboard & Packaging Materials having rich working experience of over 50 years. We are representing reputed manufacturers globally. We are well connected in the Industrial, Commercial, Public and Private Sectors. We have sales offices in Karachi and Lahore, both Business Hubs of Pakistan managed by professionally educated managers.

We are looking for Mills & / or Suppliers who have representation interest in Pakistan / Afghanistan market. For all your queries and questions, please feel free to contact us.

We are also keenly interested to cater and to associate in product development of industrial consumers.

We efficiently & confidentially source quality with reliability to the satisfaction of buyers. We solicit co-operation and support of buyers for inquires and feedback.

Services

Indenting

We have a rich experience with various mills of Asia, Europe and America as there agents in the Pakistani Market. Our deep connections in the local market allow us to provide powerful insights, enabling us to capture higher market share than our competitors.

Product Development

Our good repute and expertise in this field for over 50 years give us an edge to break the monopoly of existing brands and penetrate into the market with newer products in order to generate good sales volumes.

Imports

Our team of experts can help you identify market trends on prices of the products we offer, hence guiding you to conclude the best time to import in circumstances of uncertainty.

Customer Service

Our stakeholders are our biggest assets. We promise to provide our customers the best possible service in regards to delivering what we commit.
